Who am I

Background

After graduating in bachelor's degree in Physics at the University of Bologna in 2020, I decided to continue my studies at the same University by enrolling in the master's degree course in Applied Physics.

Things that I find interesting

Generally speaking, at the moment I am interested in various topics that (I believe) can all be connected within the physics of complex systems. In particular, until now the master's degree has allowed me to work on projects concerning dynamical systems (competitive Lotka-Volterra model, pedestrian dynamics), stochastic processes (linear noise approximation for chemical master equations) and artificial neural networks (application of variational autoencoders on biomedical data).
